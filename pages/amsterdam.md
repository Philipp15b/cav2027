<div class="lead-copy">
  <p>Amsterdam is a vibrant Dutch city known for its picturesque canals, narrow gabled houses, and lively cycling culture.</p>
  <p>The city blends centuries of history with a modern creative spirit, offering visitors charming neighborhoods, bustling cafes, and waterfront scenery at nearly every turn.</p>
</div>

Amsterdam is especially famous for its remarkable variety of museums, including the world-renowned [Rijksmuseum](https://www.rijksmuseum.nl/en), home to masterpieces by Dutch Golden Age painters such as Rembrandt and Vermeer. Art lovers also flock to the [Van Gogh Museum](https://www.vangoghmuseum.nl/en), which holds the largest collection of works by Vincent van Gogh and provides a deeply personal look into his life and artistic evolution.

One of the city's most moving historical landmarks is the [Anne Frank House](https://www.annefrank.org/en/museum/), where visitors can explore the secret annex in which Anne Frank and her family hid during the Second World War.

Beyond its famous attractions, Amsterdam offers vibrant street markets, cozy canal cruises, diverse international cuisine, and an atmosphere that feels both relaxed and culturally rich.

Amsterdam is well connected: [Amsterdam Airport Schiphol](https://www.schiphol.nl/en/) has direct flight connections to a wide variety of cities all over the world. It is also well reachable by train from many cities in Europe.
