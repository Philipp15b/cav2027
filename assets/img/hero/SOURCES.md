# Hero Image Sources

The hero images in this directory are self-hosted copies of public-domain or CC0 images from Wikimedia Commons. They have been losslessly optimized with `jpegtran -copy none -optimize -progressive`.

- `windmill.jpg`: Windmuehle.JPG, National Institute of Standards and Technology, public domain. Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Windmuehle.JPG
- `amsterdam-canal.jpg`: Canal amsterdam.JPG, public domain. Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Canal_amsterdam.JPG
- `tulips.jpg`: Keukenhof (7669851).jpg, U.S. Army photo by Sgt. 1st Class Chad Menegay, public domain. Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Keukenhof_(7669851).jpg
- `gouda.jpg`: Aged Gouda cheese.jpg, Jon Sullivan, public domain. Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Aged_Gouda_cheese.jpg
- `clogs.jpg`: Klompenmakerij Gebr Van der Velde in Best, een voorraad klompen, Bestanddeelnr 252-0766.jpg, Nationaal Archief / Willem van de Poll, CC0 1.0. Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Klompenmakerij_Gebr_Van_der_Velde_in_Best,_een_voorraad_klompen,_Bestanddeelnr_252-0766.jpg
